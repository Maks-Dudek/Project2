Welcome to Craigslist!

Craigslist is a classified advertisements website with sections devoted to jobs, housing, personals, for sale, items wanted, services, community, gigs, résumés, and discussion forums.

Founded in 1995 by Craig Newmark, Craigslist has grown to become one of the most popular websites for classified listings, with millions of users posting and browsing listings every day.

Craigslist was founded by Craig Newmark. Before making Craigslist he worked for IBM as a computer programmer. He has also worked for Charles Schwab and Bank of America. He attended Case Western Reserve University and had earned both a Bachelors and a Masters in Computer Science. His net worth is estimated to be somewhere in the 100 millions to around 1 billion dollars. Also in 2005 Time magazine listed him as one of 100 people who were shaping the world.
